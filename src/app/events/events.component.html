D'Aeroglisseur
“D'Aeroglisseur”
D'Aero Glisseur, a technical event of Technex '20, comprises the design and fabrication of a remote-controlled, wireless hovercraft or an ACV (Air-Cushioned Vehicle), which can cruise, race, move on any track and compete. A hovercraft can traverse over just about any terrain, including water, grass, ice, mud, sand, snow, or swamps. In line with this unique property of hovercrafts, the adjudicators will test the participants' designed vehicle for speed, acceleration, maneuverability and control on a path consisting of challenging terrains like smooth and rough surfaces, water, slopes, obstacles, speed breakers, and gravel.
START AT 10.00 PM
$ 30 ENTRANCE
