Drone Development Workshop

A drone, in a technological context, is an unmanned aircraft. Essentially, a drone is a flying robot. The aircrafts may be remotely controlled or can fly autonomously through software controlled flight plans in their embedded systems working in conjunction with onboard sensors and GPS. Drones can be controlled by remote control system or a ground cockpit. Drones come in a wide variety of sizes, with the large drone mostly used for military purposes such as the Predator drone, other smaller drones which can be launched by hand, to other unmanned aircraft which require short runways. WE CAN EDUCATIONAL ORGANIZATION provides an industry grade training on nano-drones discussing about each aspect of hardware and software.
