INTRODUCTION:

Gaming League is a platform where gamers can show off their gaming skills in the top selling FPS game of all time - CS:GO, in which, two teams, Terrorists and Counter-terrorists, will go up against each other. Prizes worth INR 30,000 are up for grabs!

Event Structure




Qualifier
Date of Qualifier- 14th February
This is a single elimination knockout stage.
It’s do or die. Losing teams are straight away out of the league. Winners shall keep on competing till top 4 teams remain, who will then compete in the Semi-Finales.
Semi Finale
4 teams, 2 games. This is a single elimination knockout stage.
Play to win. Lose one, you’re done. Winners head to the Grand finale. The losing teams compete against each other for the third place.
Grand Finale
The Final
In this round the top two teams, face off against each other. Three rounds, two teams, one winner, the ultimate champion.

Guidelines:

Registration fee for Gaming League is INR 1200. There will a special discount of 20% for BHU students. Registration are now open till 13th February .

Qualifier will commence on 14th February .

No new registrations will be entertained after the commencement of the Qualifier.

The Gaming League is open for all irrespective of their age or affiliation with any college. Participants can register as a team or as individuals. Teams of 5 will be formed before the Qualifier commences.

Organizers reserve the right to change the timeline and guidelines of the event.
